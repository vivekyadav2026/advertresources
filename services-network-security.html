Network Security

Perimeter defense, zero-trust segmentation, and deep packet inspection designed to halt lateral movement.

Zero-Trust Perimeter Defense

The traditional castle-and-moat approach to network security is ineffective against modern intrusion methods. Advert Resource Ltd engineers zero-trust network architectures (ZTNA) where every internal transaction is verified and authenticated.

We deploy next-generation firewalls, secure web gateways, and micro-segmentation strategies that contain breaches and prevent lateral movement through your corporate infrastructure.

Deployment Scope

  • Intrusion Detection & Prevention (IDS/IPS)
    Heuristic traffic analysis to drop malicious packets before they reach internal servers.
  • Micro-Segmentation
    Dividing the network into isolated zones to restrict access and limit breach blast-radius.
  • VPN & Access Control
    Encrypted tunneling and rigorous identity verification for remote workforce operations.
